Wigner 转动的一般情况

2025.8.19· -
Physics Relativity

Wigner 转动指出，对坐标系按序作用两个任意方向的 Lorentz boost，其等效于一个 Lorentz boost 和一个空间转动。

即

$$ \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{2}\right) \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{1}\right) = \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta} \right) \hat{R} \tag{1} $$

本文将尝试讨论一般情况下的 Wigner 转动。

相对论变换矢量关系式 #

我们首先尝试导出任意 Lorentz boost 矢量形式的变换矩阵。以 \(\bm{\beta}\) 表征 Lorentz boost ，对于在 \(S\) 中坐标为 \(( \bm{x},\ x^{0} = ct)\) 的事件，其空间坐标 \(\bm{x}\) 的平行与垂直 Lorentz boost 方向的分量可由下式给出

$$ \begin{align} \bm{x} & = x_{\parallel } \bm{e_{\parallel}} + x_{\bot} \bm{e_{\bot}} \notag \\ & = \bm{x} \cdot \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} + \left( \bm{x} - \bm{x} \cdot \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \right) \tag{2} \end{align} $$

利用 Lorentz 坐标变换公式，得

$$ \begin{align} \bm{x'} & = \gamma \left( \bm{x} \cdot \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} - \beta x^0 \right) \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} + \left( \bm{x} - \bm{x} \cdot \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \right) \notag \\ & = \left[ \mathbf{I} + (\gamma - 1) \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \right] \cdot \bm{x} - \gamma \bm{\beta} x^0 \tag{3a} \\ x'^0 & = \gamma x^0 - \bm{\beta} \gamma \cdot \bm{x} \tag{3b} \end{align} $$

可写作矩阵形式

$$ \begin{bmatrix} \bm{x'} \\ x'^0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\gamma - 1) \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} \frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} & -\bm{\beta} \gamma \\ -\bm{\beta} \gamma & \gamma \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \bm{x} \\ x^0 \end{bmatrix} \tag{4} $$

令 \(\gamma = \ch \theta\)，则 \(\beta \gamma = \sh \theta\)

再令 \(\frac{\bm{\beta}}{\beta} = \bm{e}\) 为该 Lorentz boost 方向的单位向量，故

$$ \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}) = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\ch \theta - 1) \bm{e} \bm{e} & -\sh \theta \bm{e} \\ -\sh \theta \bm{e} & \ch \theta \end{bmatrix} \tag{5} $$

确定等效 Lorentz boost \(\bm{\beta}\) #

对于按序作用两个任意方向的 Lorentz boost，记

$$ \begin{align} & \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{2}\right) \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{1}\right) \notag \\ & = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\ch \theta_2 - 1) \bm{e}_2 \bm{e}_2 & -\sh \theta_2 \bm{e}_2 \\ -\sh \theta_2 \bm{e}_2 & \ch \theta_2 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\ch \theta_1 - 1) \bm{e}_1 \bm{e}_1 & -\sh \theta_1 \bm{e}_1 \\ -\sh \theta_1 \bm{e}_1 & \ch \theta_1 \end{bmatrix} \notag \\ & = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{A}_{11} & \bm{a}_{12} \\ \bm{a}_{21} & a_{22} \end{bmatrix} \tag{6} \end{align} $$

其中

$$ \begin{align} \mathbf{A}_{11} & = \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta_1 - 1)\bm{e}_1\bm{e}_1 + (\ch\theta_2 - 1)\bm{e}_2\bm{e}_2 + \left[(\ch\theta_1 - 1)(\ch\theta_2 - 1)\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 + \sh\theta_1\sh\theta_2 \right]\bm{e}_1\bm{e}_2 \notag \quad \text{(7a)} \\ \bm{a}_{12} & = -\sh\theta_1\bm{e}_1 - \left[\sh\theta_1(\ch\theta_2 - 1)\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2 + \sh\theta_2\ch\theta_1\right]\bm{e}_2 \tag{7b} \\ \bm{a}_{21} & = -\sh\theta_2\bm{e}_2 - \left[\sh\theta_2(\ch\theta_1 - 1)\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2 + \ch\theta_2\sh\theta_1\right]\bm{e}_1 \tag{7c} \\ a_{22} & = \sh\theta_1\sh\theta_2\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2 + \ch\theta_1\ch\theta_2 \tag{7d} \end{align} $$

不妨首先看看要证明的式子

$$ \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{2}\right) \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{1}\right) = \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta} \right) \hat{R} \tag{8} $$

对左式

$$ \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{2}\right) \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta}_{1}\right) = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{A}_{11} & \bm{a}_{12} \\ \bm{a}_{21} & a_{22} \end{bmatrix} \tag{9} $$

对右式

$$ \begin{align} \hat{Q}\left(\bm{\beta} \right) \hat{R} & = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} & -\sh\theta\bm{e} \\ -\sh\theta\bm{e} & \ch\theta \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}_{11} & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \notag \\ & = \begin{bmatrix} (\mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e})\cdot \mathbf{R}_{11} & -\sh\theta\bm{e} \\ -\sh\theta\bm{e}\cdot \mathbf{R}_{11} & \ch\theta \end{bmatrix} \tag{10} \end{align} $$

且其中 \(\mathbf{R}_{11}\) 为一转动矩阵。

因此，要验证左右两式相等，可遵循如下思路

  1. 验证 \(a_{22}^2 - |\bm{a}_{12}|^2 = 1\)。这是为了能够表示出 \(\bm{\beta}\)

  2. 假设 \(\bm{\beta}\)

  3. 利用假设的 \(\bm{\beta}\) ，计算 \(\hat{R} = \hat{Q}^{-1}(\bm{\beta})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_2})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_1})\)

  4. 验证 \(\hat{R}\) 为空间转动，进而假设成立。

可验证

$$ \begin{cases} a_{22}^2 - |\bm{a}_{12}|^2 = 1 \\ a_{22}^2 - |\bm{a}_{21}|^2 = 1 \tag{11} \end{cases} $$

因此，令

$$ \begin{align} \ch\theta &= a_{22} = \sh\theta_1\sh\theta_2\bm{e}_1\cdot\bm{e}_2 + \ch\theta_1\ch\theta_2 \tag{12a} \\ \sh\theta &= |\bm{a}_{12}| = |\bm{a}_{21}| \tag{12b} \end{align} $$

对于 \(\bm{\beta}\) 的方向 \(\bm{e}\)，先假设

$$ -\sh\theta\ \ \bm{e} = \bm{a}_{12} \tag{13} $$

成立。即

$$ \bm{e} = \frac{\sh\theta_1\bm{e}_1 + \left[\sh\theta_1(\ch\theta_2 - 1)\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2 + \sh\theta_2\ch\theta_1\right]\bm{e}_2}{\sqrt{\sh^2\theta_1 \sh^2\theta_2(\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2)^2 + \ch^2\theta_1\ch^2\theta_2 + 2\sh\theta_1\sh\theta_2\ch\theta_1\ch\theta_2(\bm{e}_1\cdot \bm{e}_2) - 1}} \quad \text{(14)} $$

因此

$$ \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}) = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} & -\sh\theta\bm{e} \\ -\sh\theta\bm{e} & \ch\theta \end{bmatrix} \tag{15} $$

验证空间转动 \(\hat{R}\) #

$$ \begin{align} \hat{R} & = \hat{Q}^{-1}(\bm{\beta})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_2})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_1}) \notag \\ & = \hat{Q}(-\bm{\beta})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_2})\hat{Q}(\bm{\beta_1}) \notag \\ & = \begin{bmatrix} 1 + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} & \sh\theta\bm{e} \\ \sh\theta\bm{e} & \ch\theta \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{A}_{11} & \bm{a}_{12} \\ \bm{a}_{21} & a_{22} \end{bmatrix} \notag \\ & = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}_{11} & \bm{r}_{12} \\ \bm{r}_{21} & r_{22} \end{bmatrix} \tag{16} \end{align} $$

其中

$$ \begin{align} r_{22} & = \sh\theta\bm{e}\cdot\overline{a}_{12} + \ch\theta\cdot a_{22} \notag \\ & = \sh\theta\bm{e} \cdot (-\sh\theta\bm{e}) + \ch^2\theta \notag \\ & = -\sh^2\theta|\bm{e}|^2 + \ch^2\theta = 1 \tag{17a} \\ \bm{r}_{12} & = \left[ \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} \right] \bm{a}_{12} + \sh\theta \bm{e}\ a_{22} \notag \\ & = \left[ \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} \right] (-sh\theta \bm{e}) + sh\theta ch\theta \bm{e} \notag \\ & = \bm{0} \tag{17b} \\ \bm{r}_{21} & = \sh\theta \bm{e} \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} + \ch\theta \bm{a}_{21} \notag \\ & = -\bm{a}_{12} \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} + a_{22} \bm{a}_{21} \notag \\ & = \bm{0} \tag{17c} \\ \mathbf{R}_{11} & = \left[ \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} \right] \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} + \sh\theta \bm{e} \bm{a}_{21} \tag{17d} \\ \end{align} $$

注意到结果中 \(r_{22} = 1\), \(\bm{r}_{12}=\bm{0}\)，从而确实有 \(-\sh\theta\ \ \bm{e} = \bm{a}_{12}\) 。因此上文中的假设 (13) 是成立的。

接下来，需要验证 \(\mathbf{R}_{11}\) 确为一转动矩阵。

由于

$$ \bm{r}_{21} = \ch\theta \bm{e} \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} + \ch\theta \bm{a}_{21} = \bm{0} \tag{18} $$

从而

$$ \begin{align} \mathbf{R}_{11} & = \left[ \mathbf{I} + (\ch\theta - 1)\bm{e}\bm{e} \right] \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} + \sh\theta \bm{e} \frac{ - \sh\theta\bm{e} \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11}}{\ch\theta} \notag \\ & = \left[ \mathbf{I} - \frac{\ch\theta - 1}{\ch\theta} \bm{e}\bm{e} \right] \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} \notag \\ & = \left[ \mathbf{I} - \frac{1}{\ch\theta (\ch\theta + 1)} \bm{a}_{12} \bm{a}_{12} \right] \cdot \mathbf{A}_{11} \notag \\ & = \mathbf{I} + b_{11} \bm{e}_1 \bm{e}_1 + b_{12} \bm{e}_1 \bm{e}_2 + b_{21} \bm{e}_2 \bm{e}_1 + b_{22} \bm{e}_2 \bm{e}_2 \tag{19} \end{align} $$

现在我们已经将 \(\mathbf{R}_{11}\) 形式地表为了 (19) 式所示，即

$$ \begin{align} \mathbf{R}_{11} & = \mathbf{I} + \sum b_{ij} \bm{e}_i \bm{e}_j \notag \\ & = \mathbf{I} + \begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 & \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{12} \\ b_{21} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 \\ \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix} \tag{20} \end{align} $$

要验证其为转动矩阵，即是验证

$$ \mathbf{R}_{11} \mathbf{R}_{11}^T = \mathbf{I} \tag{21} $$

$$ \det \mathbf{R}_{11} = 1 \tag{22} $$

对于 (21) 式，利用 (20) 式，可化简为

$$ \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{12} \\ b_{21} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{21} \\ b_{12} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{12} \\ b_{21} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 \\ \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{21} \\ b_{12} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} = 0 \tag{23} $$

对于 (22) 式，考虑 (20) 式右乘 \(\begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 & \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix}\)，得到

$$ \mathbf{R}_{11} \begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 & \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 & \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix} \left( \mathbf{I} + \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{12} \\ b_{21} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 \\ \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \tag{24} \right) $$

由于

$$ \det \begin{bmatrix} \bm{e}_1 & \bm{e}_2 \end{bmatrix} = \sqrt{1 - (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)^2} \neq 0 \tag{25} $$

因此

$$ \det \mathbf{R}_{11} = \det \left( \mathbf{I} + \begin{bmatrix} b_{11} & b_{12} \\ b_{21} & b_{22} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 \\ \bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \right) = 1 \tag{26} $$

因此，需要验证 (23), (26) 式。

其中

$$ \begin{align} & b_{11} = b_{22} = - \frac{(\ch\theta_1 - 1)(\ch\theta_2 - 1)}{\ch\theta + 1} \tag{27a} \\ & b_{12} = -\frac{\sh\theta_1 \sh\theta_2}{\ch\theta + 1} \tag{27b} \\ & b_{21} = \frac{2(\ch\theta_1 - 1)(\ch\theta_2 - 1)(\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) + \sh\theta_1 \sh\theta_2}{\ch\theta + 1} \tag{27c} \\ \end{align} $$

可验证其满足关系

$$ \begin{cases} b_{11} = b_{22}\\ (b_{11}+1)(b_{22}+1) - b_{12}b_{21} = 1 \\ b_{12} + b_{21} + 2b_{22}(\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) = 0 \end{cases} \tag{28} $$

因此 \(\mathbf{R}_{11}\) 为一转动矩阵。

从而

$$ \hat{R}= \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}_{11} & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \tag{29} $$

对应空间转动。

转动矩阵 \(\mathbf{R}_{11}\) 的矢量形式 #

首先对单位元 \(\mathbf{I}\) 进行分解。

设 \(\mathbf{I} = C_{11} \bm{e}_1\bm{e}_1 + C_{12} \bm{e}_1\bm{e}_2 + C_{21} \bm{e}_2\bm{e}_1 + C_{22} \bm{e}_2\bm{e}_2\)

则

$$ C_{11} = C_{22} = \frac{1}{1 - (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)^2} \tag{30} $$

$$ C_{12} = C_{21} = -\frac{\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2}{1 - (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)^2} \tag{31} $$

从而

$$ \begin{align} \mathbf{R}_{11} &= \mathbf{I} + \frac{b_{11}}{C_{11}} + \left( b_{11} - b_{11}\frac{C_{11}}{C_{11}} \right) \bm{e}_1\bm{e}_1 + \left( b_{12} - b_{11}\frac{C_{12}}{C_{11}} \right) \bm{e}_1\bm{e}_2 \notag \\ &\phantom{{}= \mathbf{I} + \frac{b_{11}}{C_{11}}} + \left( b_{21} - b_{11}\frac{C_{21}}{C_{11}} \right) \bm{e}_2\bm{e}_1 + \left( b_{22} - b_{11}\frac{C_{22}}{C_{11}} \right) \bm{e}_2\bm{e}_2 \notag \\ &= \left[ \alpha (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) + \beta \right] \mathbf{I} + \alpha \left[ \bm{e}_2\bm{e}_1 - \bm{e}_1\bm{e}_2 \right] \tag{32} \end{align} $$

或

$$ (\mathbf{R}_{11})_{ik} = \left[ \alpha (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) + \beta \right] \delta_{ik} + \alpha \epsilon_{ijk} (\bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2)_j \tag{33} $$

其中

$$ \begin{cases} \alpha = \frac{\sh\theta_1\sh\theta_2 + (\ch\theta_1-1)(\ch\theta_2-1)(\bm{e_1} \cdot \bm{e_2})}{\ch\theta+1} \\ \beta = \frac{\ch\theta_1+\ch\theta_2}{\ch\theta+1} \end{cases} \tag{34} $$

记转动的转角为 \(\phi\)，转动的方向向量为 \(\bm{n}\)，因此

$$ \begin{aligned} \bm{n} \sin\phi & = \alpha (\bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2) \notag \\ & = \frac{\sh\theta_1 \sh\theta_2 + (\ch\theta_1 - 1)(\ch\theta_2 - 1)(\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)}{\ch\theta + 1} \bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2 \notag \\ & = \frac{\sh\theta_1 \sh\theta_2 + (\ch\theta_1 - 1)(\ch\theta_2 - 1)(\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)}{\sh\theta_1 \sh\theta_2 (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) + \ch\theta_1 \ch\theta_2 + 1} \bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2 \quad \text{(35)} \end{aligned} $$

补充 #

（一）Thomas 进动 #

考虑 \(\beta_1, \beta_2 \to 0\) 时的低阶近似
此时

$$ \begin{align} \bm{n} \sin\phi & = \frac{\gamma_1 \gamma_2 \beta_1 \beta_2 + (\gamma_1 - 1)(\gamma_2 - 1)(\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2)}{\gamma_1 \gamma_2 \beta_1 \beta_2 (\bm{e}_1 \cdot \bm{e}_2) + \gamma_1 \gamma_2 + 1} \bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2 \notag \\ & \approx \frac{1}{2} \beta_1 \beta_2 \bm{e}_1 \times \bm{e}_2 \notag \\ & = \frac{1}{2} \bm{\beta}_1 \times \bm{\beta}_2 \tag{36} \end{align} $$

因此可近似 \(\sin\phi \approx \phi\)，得到转角

$$ \bm{n} \phi = \frac{1}{2} \bm{\beta}_1 \times \bm{\beta}_2 \tag{37} $$

例如，对氢原子轨道电子的 Thomas 进动

$$ \bm{\beta}_1 = \frac{\bm{v}}{c}, \quad \bm{\beta}_2 = \frac{{\bm{a}} dt}{c} \tag{38} $$

故

$$ \bm{n} d\phi = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\bm{v} \times {\bm{a}}}{c^2} dt \tag{39} $$

即

$$ \bm{\omega} = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\bm{v} \times {\bm{a}}}{c^2} \tag{40} $$

带入 Bohr 氢原子轨道模型，得

$$ \omega = \frac{\mu_0}{8\pi} \frac{m e^8}{(4\pi \epsilon_0)^3 (n \hbar)^5} \tag{41} $$

（二）代数结构 #

Wigner 转动来自于 Lorentz boost 生成元 \(\hat{k}_i\) 的非零对易子。

设两次 Lorentz boost 快度分别为 \(\bm{\eta}^1, \bm{\eta}^2\)，即

$$ \bm{\eta}^1 = (\sh^{-1} \beta_{1}\gamma_1) \frac{\bm{\beta}_1}{\beta_1} \tag{42} $$

$$ \bm{\eta}^2 = (\sh^{-1} \beta_{2}\gamma_2) \frac{\bm{\beta}_2}{\beta_2} \tag{43} $$

故

$$ \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}_1) = \exp\left(i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}} \right) \tag{44} $$

$$ \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}_2) = \exp\left(i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}\right) \tag{45} $$

由于

$$ \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}_2) \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}_1) = \hat{Q}(\bm{\beta}) \hat{R} \tag{46} $$

即

$$ \exp(i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \exp(i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) = \exp(i \bm{\eta} \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \hat{R} \tag{47} $$

根据对称性，有

$$ \exp(i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \exp(i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) = \exp(i \bm{\eta} \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \hat{R}^{-1} \tag{48} $$

因此

$$ \exp(i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \exp(i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) = \exp(i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \exp(i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}) \hat{R}^2 \tag{49} $$

由 Baker-Campbell-Hausdorff 公式，有

$$ \hat{R}^2 = \exp\left( \left[i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}, i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}\right] + \frac{1}{2}\left[ \left[i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}, i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}} \right], i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}} + i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}\right] + \cdots \right) \quad \text{(50)} $$

考虑 \(\beta_1, \beta_2 \to 0\) 的低阶近似

$$ \begin{align} \hat{R}^2 & \approx \exp\left(\left[i \bm{\eta}^2 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}, i \bm{\eta}^1 \cdot \hat{\bm{K}}\right]\right) \notag \\ & =\exp\left(\left[i {\eta}^2_i \hat{K}_i, i {\eta}^1_j \hat{K}_j \right]\right) \notag \\ & =\exp\left(- {\eta}^2_i {\eta}^1_j \left[\hat{K}_i, \hat{K}_j \right]\right) \notag \\ & = \exp\left(- {\eta}^2_i {\eta}^1_j \epsilon_{ijk} \hat{J}_k \right) \notag \\ & = \exp\left(- (\bm{\eta}^1 \times \bm{\eta}^2 ) \cdot \hat{\bm{J}} \right) \tag{51} \end{align} $$

故

$$ \hat{R} \approx \exp\left(- \frac{1}{2} (\bm{\eta}^1 \times \bm{\eta}^2 ) \cdot \hat{\bm{J}} \right) \tag{52} $$

转角

$$ \bm{n} \phi \approx \frac{1}{2} \bm{\eta}^1 \times \bm{\eta}^2 \approx \frac{1}{2} \bm{\beta}_1 \times \bm{\beta}_2 \tag{53} $$
